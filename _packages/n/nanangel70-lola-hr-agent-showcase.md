## 📋 About lola-hr-agent-showcase

Lola is an AI-powered tool designed to help manage HR contracts automatically. It focuses on making contract workflows clear and easy to follow while keeping track of every step for later review. This system supports professional teams by handling detailed tasks in hiring and HR, ensuring every contract follows rules and can be audited.

With Lola, users can reduce manual effort, avoid errors, and keep their contract processes well-organized without needing deep technical knowledge.

## 💡 How to Use Lola

Once you open Lola, here’s how you can get started managing your contracts:

1. **Upload Contracts:** Use the clear button to select contract files from your computer. Lola supports common formats like PDF and Word documents.

2. **Set Workflow Options:** Choose the steps you want the AI to follow. For example: review contract terms, check for compliance, or set approval reminders.

3. **Run the Automation:** Click 'Start' to let Lola process the contracts. The AI works quietly in the background to analyze and organize your contracts.

4. **Review Results:** Once finished, the program shows a clear summary of each contract’s status. You can view audit trails that keep track of what happened at each step.

5. **Save or Export:** Export reports and audit logs as files or send them to your team for review.
